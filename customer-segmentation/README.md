# Customer Segmentation

## Overview  
Customer segmentation is the practice of dividing a company's customers into groups based on common characteristics. This helps in tailoring marketing strategies and improving customer engagement.

## Objectives  
- To identify distinct segments within the customer base.  
- To understand the preferences and behaviors of each segment.  
- To optimize marketing strategies based on segment characteristics.

## Methods  
- Data collection through surveys, purchase history, and customer feedback.  
- Applying clustering algorithms such as K-means and hierarchical clustering.  
- Using visualization tools to present the findings.

## Expected Outcomes  
- Clear identification of customer segments.  
- Insights into customer preferences and behaviors.  
- Recommendations for targeted marketing strategies.
